Spirit Rally Commissioner

I have been Spirit Rally Commissioner since my freshman year. The job of a Spirit Rally Commissioner is to help prepare for a school-wide event. I organized dates and collaborate with the Class of 2024 to prepare for decorations, a musical-like play, and a dance show. The planning takes up to two month. On the Spirit Rally Day, everyone arrives to school early to show their school spirit and to outcompete the other grade classes.
